About Me

I am a second-year ECE student at UT Austin interested in Software Development and Embedded Systems. I have exposure to a variety of topics, including biomedical devices, Android app development, C, Python, and Java.

On The Map

On The Map is an Android app I developed (and will continue to work on) with a group of friends at the HackTogether hackathon. The purpose of the app was to allow for small-scale events, such as groups tabling on the UT campus with free food, to be made more visible, or placed "On The Map"

Autonomous Car Project

Project for EE302 class using an Arduino, photoresistors, and distance sensor to make a car that can follow a line and stop before an obstacle. Scored 100.

Austin BCycle Path Data Based On Membership Type

Part of our MLDS Datahack project. Using Python to traverse the dataset, we created graphical representations of our findings from the data. For complete conclusions, see my Github.
